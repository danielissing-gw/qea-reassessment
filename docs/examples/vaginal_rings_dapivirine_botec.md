## Infections prevented

| Parameter | Value | Source / notes |
|---|---|---|
| Baseline rate of new HIV incidence | 5% | For KP programs, we model 1.9–9.6% in SSA. Using a rough midpoint here. [Incidence estimates](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vH1Fh9TUotkKvIeX7uR9-hVHKqrGJJwBQLugb8rkDdA/edit?gid=2020638865#gid=2020638865&range=23:23) |
| Risk reduction for HIV acquisition, dapivirine ring compared with placebo | 0.505 | Average of [Baeten 2022 (HOPE)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8038210/) (39%) and [Nel 2021 (DREAM)](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lanhiv/article/PIIS2352-3018(20)30300-3/fulltext) (62%). Nel 2021: "18 (1.9%) HIV-1 infections were confirmed during DVR use, resulting in an incidence of 1.8 (95% CI 1.1–2.6) per 100 person-years, 62% lower than the simulated placebo rate." Baeten 2022: "HIV-1 incidence was 2.7 per 100 person-years (95% CI 1.9–3.8, n=35 infections), compared to an expected incidence of 4.4 per 100 person-years (95% CI 3.2–5.8) among a population matched on age, site, and presence of a sexually transmitted infection from the placebo group of ASPIRE." 1 − 2.7/4.4 ≈ 39%. |
| IV adjustment | 95% | Use same values as for [LEN](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vH1Fh9TUotkKvIeX7uR9-hVHKqrGJJwBQLugb8rkDdA/edit?gid=1203937207#gid=1203937207) |
| EV adjustment | 90% | Use same values as for [LEN](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vH1Fh9TUotkKvIeX7uR9-hVHKqrGJJwBQLugb8rkDdA/edit?gid=1203937207#gid=1203937207) |
| Penalty for repetitive saving (infections merely delayed) | −25% | See [estimate for MOZ](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vH1Fh9TUotkKvIeX7uR9-hVHKqrGJJwBQLugb8rkDdA/edit?gid=2020638865#gid=2020638865&range=23:23), where incidence is estimated at 4.7%, similar to what we assume here. Might be lower for non-KPs (e.g. AGYW) |
| Additional infections averted per infection averted directly by PrEP | 0.6 | Average guess for SSA, [see here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vH1Fh9TUotkKvIeX7uR9-hVHKqrGJJwBQLugb8rkDdA/edit?gid=2020638865#gid=2020638865&range=23:23) |
| Infections prevented by Dapivirine ring (lifetime) | ~26 | Calculation |
